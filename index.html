About Me
A developer,
a teacher,
a builder

I started learning web development in 2023 at Larva Coding Academy. That same year, after completing my training, I was recruited as a tutor there — I went from student to teacher in months. That experience shaped everything.

Since then, I've worked on countless projects — from websites to web apps to brand identities. In 2024, I was privileged to teach at a federal college in Ibadan, further deepening my passion for education and knowledge transfer.

In 2025, I picked up French because I fell in love with the language. And now in 2026, I'm building mcode — an online tech school born from a simple truth: a lot of people want to learn tech but can't afford it, and when they try to teach themselves, they get stuck. I'm changing that.
